How does this website work?
back to top

Amazon sellers publish FREE SAMPLES here to encourage buyers to experience their products.

As a buyer, follow the steps below to get your unlimited free samples.

1. Browse and select products you like on our site. Place order on Amazon.

2. Try out your new purchase. Share your honest product review on Amazon.

3. Submit your review link and paypal account to us. We will rebate full product price into your Paypal account in one business day.

When and how can I get the rebate?
back to top

After you submit the review link, the rebate will be sent to your paypal account in one business day.

Is the rebate guaranteed?
back to top

The rebated is reserved in our escrow account, so it's 100% guaranteed.

Does this rebate exchange for review model comply with Amazon regulation?
back to top

Amazon has its own Vine Program providing free products to high ranked reviewers.

We believe every reviewer deserves free samples as long as his/her reviews are unbiased and valuable.

How to leave reviews?
back to top

(1) Log in your Amazon account.

(2) Go to Your orders.

(3) Find out the product and click the button "Write a product review".

How to submit reviews?
back to top

Log in our website, go to page ‘Deal Manager’and then submit the review link.

How to find the correct review link?
back to top

step 1: find the review and click the title

step 2: copy the url and then submit it in our website

Is 5-star review required?
back to top

NO, it is not required. We encourage you to write honest and helpful reviews.

Is there any requirement of the review?
back to top

Yes. It should be your real experience of the product and it should be a verified purchase review.

What can I do if there is no verified purchased sign on my review?
back to top

We only send rebate for VP reviews.

There are two options. Keep it if you like it or return it to Amazon if you don’t like it.

When does website update the site with new products?
back to top

1. New product releasing could happen at any time, because we are trying to get good deal to shopper/reviewer as soon as possible.

2. Some deals may dispear due to daily quota, but it will be back the next day.

back to top

Seller may get access to your other inform from other channels after you made the purchase, for example through Amazon seller's account.

How to increase the deal limit?
back to top

Deal limit depends on your reviewer level. The higher level you are, the more deals you can request. For example, you can request at most 3 deals if your level is 3 while you can request 5 deals if your level is 5.

To get higher level, you just need earn review & rebate points by providing high quality reviews. You level would be upgraded by one for each ten points. High quality review here means:

1. Detailed review. You can get one point if submit a review with more than 200 characters.

2. Review with photo or video. You can get one point if submit a review with photos or videos.

But, if your review submitted is poor quality, your points would be decreased.

1. Review removed. Your points will be decreased by two if review is removed by Amazon.

2. Review too short. Your points will be decreased by one if review is too short.

3. Review plagiarized. Your points will be decreased by one if review is same to others.

What can I do if I reach to the highest level 8?
back to top

Since Amazon don’t allow people write reviews too frequently. I limit the highest level to 8. We will provide more benefit for your accumulated points.

What does the error 'The review is not verified purchased' mean?
back to top

Usually if you buy the product from Amazon and then write a review, there will be a sign 'verified purchase' on your review.

But occasionaly, Amazon miss to put the verified purchase sign on review even you bought from Amazon.

What does the error 'The reivew is not written by you' mean?
back to top

That means the review author's Amazon profile is different to your profile recorded in our system when you signed up.
